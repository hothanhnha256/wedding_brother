Axie Infinity is a virtual world filled with cute, formidable creatures known as axies. Axie was built to introduce the world to Blockchain through a fun, nostalgic, & charming game.

Ronin

Ronin is an EVM blockchain crafted for developers building games with player-owned economies. Ronin is the #1 gaming-centric blockchain by all-time NFT volume.
